Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  5. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 pound pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add pancetta or bacon and cook until crisp.
  3. In a bowl, beat together eggs, grated cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving some pasta water. Immediately add hot spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta, tossing to coat.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Milk Tart

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon, for dusting
  • 1 ready-made pie crust (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine milk, sugar, and butter. Heat until butter melts and mixture is warm.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk into warm milk mixture.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into a pie crust (optional) or serving dish. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator until set, about 2 hours.
  7. Slice and serve chilled.

Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Warm milk in a small saucepan until it bubbles, then remove from heat. Mix in butter; stir until melted. Add sugar and salt, and stir until dissolved. Let cool until lukewarm.
  2. In a large bowl, combine lukewarm milk mixture with egg and yeast. Stir in 2 cups of flour and beat until smooth. Add remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring well after each addition. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth, about 8 minutes.
  3. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise until doubled in bulk, about 1 hour.
  4. Punch down dough and roll out to 1/2 inch thickness. Cut into circles with a cookie cutter or drinking glass. Let doughnuts sit out to rise again until double. Cover loosely with a cloth.
  5. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large heavy skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Slide doughnuts into the hot oil using a wide spatula. Turn doughnuts over as they rise to the surface. Fry doughnuts on each side until golden brown. Remove from hot oil, to drain on a wire rack.
  6. Dust with confectioners' sugar while they are still warm, and serve immediately.

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, yogurt, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just blended.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  5. Serve hot with maple syrup, fruit, or other toppings of your choice.
